IT'S IMPACTS !

Illegal logging destroys trees that act as carbon sinks and climate regulators which play a significant role in Global Warming and Climate Change.
Cutting trees both adds carbon dioxide to the air and removes the ability to absorb existing carbon dioxide. ⯇ ⯈
